What We Offer

Clinical Excellence,
Complete Care

Visual Acuity Testing

Precise measurement of near and far vision using calibrated charts and optometric tools — the foundation of your prescription.

Comprehensive Eye Exam ★

Our signature full-spectrum examination: retinal health, refraction, pressure testing, and complete ocular analysis in one visit.

Pediatric Eye Care

Gentle, thorough examinations for children — detecting vision problems early for healthy development and academic confidence.

Dry Eye Assessment

Advanced tear film analysis with personalized management plans — including environmental and lifestyle recommendations.

Fundus Photography

High-resolution retinal imaging to monitor ocular health over time and enable early detection of serious eye conditions.

Cataract Screening

Slit-lamp biomicroscopy for early detection of cataracts and lens opacities — giving you a head start on your eye health journey.
